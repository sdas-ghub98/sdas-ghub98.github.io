About

With over 6 years of experience in application security, penetration testing, and threat modeling, I specialize in driving enterprise security maturity and maximizing the ROI of AppSec investments. Operating at the intersection of technical execution and business strategy, I design scalable DevSecOps workflows, seamlessly integrating security testing (SAST, DAST, SCA) into complex CI/CD pipelines, SCMs, and IDEs across AWS, Azure, and GCP environments. I partner directly with enterprise clients and executive teams to conduct comprehensive AppSec health assessments - analyzing vulnerability density, remediation velocity, and platform adoption to identify risky applications and operational bottlenecks. By leveraging data-driven insights, I guide organizations through critical on-prem to cloud platform migrations and ensure optimal security posture. As a forward-thinking architect, I am actively pioneering the integration of Agentic AI into operational security, defining new service capabilities and engineering automated, high-efficiency workflows using advanced LLMs like Claude.
